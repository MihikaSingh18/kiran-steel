About Our Team

Drawing on their extensive experience in the metal industry, Kiran Steel India store provides premium, high-quality metal solutions to businesses across India. Their shared expertise in metallurgy and unwavering commitment to excellence have quickly established Kiran Steel as a trusted name in the industry.

The team witnessed firsthand the challenges that companies faced in sourcing dependable metal products. This shared experience inspired them to establish Kiran Steel, with a focus on precision cutting, custom fabrication, and on-time delivery.

Under our leadership, the company has grown from a small operation to serving major industrial clients across Maharashtra and beyond, all while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction.
